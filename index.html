About This Fork

This edition is highly inspired by the original webcam-controlled brick-breaker idea, but rebuilt as a self-contained offline project with local dependencies, local score storage, cleaner structure, and a sharper arcade presentation.

Nothing leaves your machine during play. Camera frames stay in the browser, score history stays in local storage, and the runtime no longer depends on external CDNs.

Controls are simple: keep your palm visible, slide left or right, and catch the ball with the paddle. Each cleared board increases speed and shrinks the paddle a bit, so the game gets meaner the longer you survive.
